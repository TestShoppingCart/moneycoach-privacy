Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 18, 2026

Moniqo is an expense tracking app that helps you understand and manage your spending. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Mobile number – required for login and account identification
  • Email address – optional, used for account recovery
  • Expense data – transactions you enter (amount, category, merchant, date)
  • Device ID – auto-generated to link your data to your device

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To store and display your expense history
  • To generate spending insights and summaries
  • To identify your account across sessions

3. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Data is stored securely on our backend servers
  • We do not store passwords

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to any third parties. We have no advertising networks, no analytics services, and no data brokers. Your data stays private.

5. Account Deletion Play Store Required

You can permanently delete your account at any time directly from the app:

  • Open Moniqo → go to Profile tab
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion
  • All your data will be permanently removed from our servers immediately

6. Children's Privacy

Moniqo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
